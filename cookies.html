Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how SargamWorld (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze usage, and support our website's functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Some cookies are essential, while others are optional and require your consent.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

SargamWorld uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies (Always Active): These cookies enable core features like page navigation, security, session management, and remembering your cookie settings. Without these, the website cannot function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: If enabled, these cookies help us understand how users interact with the site (page views, clicks, device type). Example: Google Analytics.
  • Advertising Cookies: If enabled in the future, these cookies help deliver personalized ads and measure ad performance. Example: Google AdSense (planned).

3. How We Collect Consent

When you visit SargamWorld for the first time, a cookie banner appears allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Enable only selected categories
  • Manage cookies later anytime from the footer link

Your preferences are stored in your browser through localStorage, not cookies, to maintain privacy and avoid storing unnecessary data.

4. How to Change or Withdraw Consent

You can update your cookie choices at any time by clicking the “Manage Cookies” button in the footer.

You may also clear all cookies and stored data from your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data
  • Safari: Settings → Clear History and Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data

5. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include embedded content and third-party scripts. These providers may set cookies based on their own privacy policies.

Examples:

  • YouTube (video embeds)
  • Google Analytics (analytics)
  • Google AdSense (ads — planned)

6. Data Retention

Your cookie preferences are stored until you clear them manually from your browser or change your selection using the “Manage Cookies” button.
