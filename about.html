About SargamWorld

Discover our mission, story, and vision for making Indian Classical Music accessible worldwide.

Our Mission

At SargamWorld, our mission is to preserve and share the beauty of Indian classical music, providing easy-to-follow notations, theory, and resources for learners of all levels.

Our Story

What started as a small passion project grew into a platform for enthusiasts around the globe. From raags to taals, our collection continues to expand with the goal of supporting learners and teachers alike.

Vision

We envision building a community where music lovers can not only explore notations but also connect with experts and each other to keep traditions alive in the digital age.
