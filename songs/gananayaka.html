Shree Ganeshay Dheemahi

Movie: Viruddh… Family Comes First (2005) · Singer: Shankar Mahadevan · Music: Ajay–Atul

Understanding Swar & Octaves

  • Shuddha Swar: Sa Re Ga Pa Dha Ni
  • Komal Swar:(Ga) (Dha) (Ni)
  • Octaves:
    • .Sa, .Re, .Ga = lower octave
    • Sa, Re, Ga = middle octave
    • Sa’, Re’, Ga’ = higher octave
  • Notes in { }: Murki (fast turn)
  • ~: Meend (glide)
  • ( ): Kann swar (touch note)
