Pratham Tula Vandito

Movie: Ashtavinayak (1979) · Singer: Dr. Vasantrao Deshpande

Understanding Swar & Notation

  • Shuddha Swaras: Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni
  • Komal Swaras: (k)
  • Teevra Swara: # – e.g., Ma#
  • Octaves:
    • .Sa, .Re = Mandra Saptak
    • Sa, Re = Madhya Saptak
    • Sa’, Re’ = Taar Saptak
