🎼 Understanding Swar & Notation

  • Shuddha Swaras: Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni
  • Komal Swaras: (k) – e.g., G(k), D(k)
  • Teevra Swara: (T) – e.g., M(T)
  • Octaves:
    • .S, .R = Mandra Saptak
    • S, R = Madhya Saptak
    • S’, R’ = Taar Saptak
