Lag Ja Gale

Movie: Woh Kaun Thi (1964) · Singer: Lata Mangeshkar · Lyrics: Raja Mehdi Ali Khan · Music: Madan Mohan

Understanding Swar Notation

  • Shuddha Swar (Natural): Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni
  • Komal Swar (Flat): (Ga), (Ni)
  • Tivra Swar (Sharp): Ma#
  • Octaves:
    • .Sa, .Re, .Ga = Mandra Saptak (lower octave)
    • Sa, Re, Ga = Madhya Saptak (middle octave)
    • Sa’, Re’, Ga’ = Taara Saptak (higher octave)
