O Mere Dil Ke Chain

Movie: Mere Jeevan Saathi (1972) · Singer: Kishore Kumar · Lyrics: Majrooh Sultanpuri · Music: R. D. Burman

Understanding Swar & Octaves

  • Shuddha Swar: Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Dha Ni
  • Komal/Tivra Swar: none here
  • Octaves:
    • .Sa, .Re, .Ga = lower octave
    • Sa, Re, Ga = middle octave
    • Sa’, Re’, Ga’ = higher octave
  • Meend ~: glide between notes
  • Notes in { }: Murki (fast turn)
