🎼 Understanding Swar & Notation

  • Shuddha Swaras: सा रे ग म प ध नि
  • Komal Swaras: (K) e.g. G(K), D(K)
  • Teevra Swara: (T) e.g. M(T)
  • Octaves:
    • .S, .R, .G = Mandra Saptak (lower)
    • S, R, G = Madhya Saptak (middle)
    • S’, R’, G’ = Taar Saptak (higher)
