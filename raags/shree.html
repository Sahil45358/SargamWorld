Raag Shree

Thaat:

Kalyan

Jaati:

Shadav–Shadav

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: Re
Samvadi: Dha

Aroha:

S R G M P D N S’

Avroha:

S’ N D P M G R S

Time of Singing:

Evening (6 PM – 9 PM)

Nature:

Majestic, devotional, serene

Special Notes:

Raag Shree prominently features Komal Dha and Komal Ni. It evokes a royal and devotional atmosphere and is often performed at the opening of classical concerts.
