Raag Kedar

Thaat:

Kalyan

Jaati:

Audav – Sampurna

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: Ma
Samvadi: Sa

Aroha:

S M, M P D P N S’

Avroha:

S’ N D P M, M R S

Time of Singing:

Late evening / Night

Nature:

Devotional, serene, expansive

Special Notes:

Raag Kedar is known for its graceful meends and oscillation between shuddha and tivra Ma. It creates a calm, devotional atmosphere and is often sung in vilambit tempo.
