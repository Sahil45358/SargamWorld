Raag Darbari Kanada

Thaat:

Bhairavi

Jaati:

Sampurna – Sampurna

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: Re
Samvadi: Pa

Aroha:

S R G M P D N S’

Avroha:

S’ n d n P M G r S

Time of Singing:

Late Night

Nature:

Majestic, introspective, expressive

Special Notes:

Renowned for its slow, meend-rich phrases. Features Komal Ga, Ni, Dha and subtle oscillations (gamak) on Re and Dha.
