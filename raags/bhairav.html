Raag Bhairav

Thaat:

Bhairav

Jaati:

Sampurna – Sampurna

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: Dha
Samvadi: Re

Aroha:

S r G M P d N S’

Avroha:

S’ N d P M G r S

Time of Singing:

Early morning (4 AM – 7 AM)

Nature:

Serious, devotional, meditative

Special Notes:

Raag Bhairav uses both Komal Re and Komal Dha. It is one of the oldest and most powerful morning raags.
