Raag Charukeshi

Thaat:

Charukeshi (Carnatic)—approx. Bhairavi in Hindustani

Jaati:

Sampurna – Sampurna

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: G
Samvadi: D

Aroha:

S R G M P D N S’

Avroha:

S’ N D P M G R S

Time of Singing:

Evening

Nature:

Devotional, serene, expressive

Special Notes:

Charukeshi (Carnatic rāgam) corresponds closely to Bhairavi and is widely used in South Indian classical music, devotional and light compositions.
