Raag Jog

Thaat:

Bhairav

Jaati:

Audav – Audav

Vadi & Samvadi:

Vadi: Ga
Samvadi: Ni

Aroha:

S G r m P N S’

Avroha:

S’ N P m r G S

Time of Singing:

Late Night

Nature:

Mystical, romantic, poignant

Special Notes:

Jog omits Dha and uses Komal Re and Komal Ni. It evokes a dreamy and ethereal mood, popular in light classical and devotional renditions.
